About Me

Hi there! I am Atul Anand from Hyderabad, India. I currently work as a Project Lead. While at different points in my career I have worked as a UI developer, middleware developer and back end developer, but my expertise lies in middleware where I have the maximum exposure.

I have over 10 years experience working in middleware technologies like TIBCO, IBM ESB, IBM WPS, IBM DataPower.

Recently started exploring on other middleware products like Mule 4 and Dell Boomi AtomSphere. With having prior integration experience, it was quite comfortable learning Mule 4 and DellBoomi. I completed the developer and architect certification for it.

In my leisure time I like spending time with my son, travelling, playing sports and watching movies.
